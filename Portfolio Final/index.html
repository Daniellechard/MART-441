About

I'm a graphic designer based in the heart of Missoula, MT. As a graphic and brand designer it's my job to take the visions you have and turn them into visuals you can use.

My love for design and aesthetics has been a core part of me ever since I was a child. Whether it was scrapbooking with my mom, making cards, or building miniature furniture for my dollhouse, I was always busy making something. As I grew up, my interest in design grew as well. I wasn't sure if I wanted to pursue the arts in college because everyone knows the "starving artist" stereotype, but after taking my first couple of digital arts classes in my Sophomore year I was instantly hooked.

My mission as a designer is simple, to serve people through design and to do so with empathy and compassion.
